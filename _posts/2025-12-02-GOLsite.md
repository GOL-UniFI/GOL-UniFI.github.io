On December 1st and 11th we had three seminars from the FLorence Optimization Series: 
John Pearson from University of Edinburgh talked about “Recent Developments in Numerical Methods for PDE-Constrained Optimization”
Tizian Wenzel from LMU Munich talked about “Efficient and Adaptive Kernel Models for Machine Learning”
Simone Rebegoldi from Università degli studi di Modena e Reggio Emilia talked about "On the convergence and stability of multiscale decompositions for imaging inverse problems"

Check the [FLorence Optimization Series website](https://sites.google.com/unifi.it/floptalks/home?authuser=3){:target="_blank"} to keep you updated to the next seminars!
