We are pleased to announce the publication of our work “Efficient Globalization of Heavy-Ball Type Methods for Unconstrained Optimization Based on Curve Searches” in Journal of Optimization Theory and Applications. We are very happy for our researchers [Federica Donnini](https://webgol.dinfo.unifi.it/pages/federica_donnini/), [Matteo Lapucci](https://webgol.dinfo.unifi.it/pages/matteo_lapucci/) and [Pierluigi Mansueto](https://webgol.dinfo.unifi.it/pages/pierluigi_mansueto/) on this achievement!

The paper is available [here](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10957-026-03035-w).
